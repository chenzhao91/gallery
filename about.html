About

趙辰，1991年生，中國天津人，現居美國喬治亞州亞特蘭大。

2011年，一次從天津去北戴河的路途中，山間忽起雲海，美得令人屏息。彼時手中只有一部 iPhone 4，無力留住眼前所見。那一刻的遺憾，成了拿起相機的起點。

此後入手人生第一台單反 Nikon D7100，開始學著用快門捕捉光影。2018年赴美後，被這片土地上廣袤的自然風光所震撼，轉入 Sony 無反系統，從此一發不可收。

雪山、瀑布、星空、大海、沙漠、曠野——凡目之所及，皆想以鏡頭留存。

對我而言，攝影即生活——是對美的記錄，是成長的腳步，也是對回憶的定格與珍藏。

Chen Zhao. Born 1991 in Tianjin, China. Living in Atlanta, GA.

It started with a regret — clouds rolling through mountains on a summer drive, too beautiful for the phone in my hand to hold. That was 2011. I've been chasing that feeling ever since.

First with a Nikon DSLR, learning light the slow way. Then across the Pacific, where the American wilderness cracked something open. Sony mirrorless. Wide lenses. Long roads.

I photograph what stops me — snow on granite, stars over desert, the hour when the sky can't decide what color it wants to be.

Every frame is a negotiation between what I saw and what I felt. The camera rarely wins. But sometimes it comes close.
